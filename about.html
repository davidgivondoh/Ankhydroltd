Our Story

Empowering Kenya with Clean Energy & Water

ANK Hydro Limited is a Kenyan-based company headquartered in Kitui Town, specializing in solar powering solutions, borehole drilling, pump systems, and irrigation services.

We serve residential, commercial, agricultural, and institutional clients across Kenya with a strong focus on quality, transparency, and dependable after-sales support. Our philosophy is simple: "Our Customers are our priority."

From the first consultation to long-term maintenance, we are committed to delivering solutions that work — powered by the right technology, installed by experts, and backed by 24/7 support.
