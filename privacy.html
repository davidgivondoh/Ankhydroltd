Effective Date: March 2026

ANK Hydro Limited ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard data when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

  • Personal details you provide via forms (name, phone, email, location)

How We Use Your Information
